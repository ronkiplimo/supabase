# Supabase Pricing

> Start for free, scale as you grow. Pay only for what you use.

Supabase offers four plans: Free, Pro, Team, and Enterprise. All plans include unlimited API requests. Pro and Team plans include one project running on Micro compute ($10/month value) in the base price.

Note: prices shown are as of April 2026. For current pricing, visit https://supabase.com/pricing.

## Plan Tiers

### Free - $0/month
- 2 active projects (paused after 1 week of inactivity)
- 50,000 monthly active users (MAUs)
- 500 MB database size (Shared CPU, 500 MB RAM)
- 5 GB egress, 5 GB cached egress
- 1 GB file storage
- 500,000 Edge Function invocations
- 200 Realtime concurrent connections
- 2 million Realtime messages
- 1-day log retention
- Community support

### Pro - from $25/month
- 100,000 MAUs included, then $0.00325 per additional MAU
- 8 GB database disk included, then $0.125 per GB
- 250 GB bandwidth included, then $0.09 per GB
- 100 GB file storage included, then $0.021 per GB
- 2 million Edge Function invocations, then $2 per million
- 500 Realtime concurrent connections, then $10 per 1,000
- 5 million Realtime messages, then $2.50 per million
- 7-day log retention
- Daily backups (7-day retention)
- Email support

### Team - from $599/month
- Same usage quotas and overage rates as Pro
- SOC2 compliance
- HIPAA available as paid add-on
- SSO for Supabase Dashboard
- Project-scoped and read-only access controls
- Priority email support with SLAs
- Daily backups (14-day retention)
- 28-day log retention

### Enterprise - custom pricing
- Designated support manager
- Uptime SLAs
- Bring Your Own Cloud (BYO Cloud)
- 24x7x365 premium support
- Private Slack channel
- Custom security questionnaires
- 90-day log retention

## Compute Add-Ons

All projects run on a compute instance. Pro and Team include Micro compute in the base price.

|------|---------|-----|-----------|-----|-------------------|-------------------|
| Micro | $10 | 2-core ARM | Shared | 1 GB | 60 | 200 |
| Small | $15 | 2-core ARM | Shared | 2 GB | 90 | 400 |
| Medium | $60 | 2-core ARM | Shared | 4 GB | 120 | 600 |
| Large | $110 | 2-core ARM | Dedicated | 8 GB | 160 | 800 |
| XL | $210 | 4-core ARM | Dedicated | 16 GB | 240 | 1,000 |
| 2XL | $410 | 8-core ARM | Dedicated | 32 GB | 380 | 1,500 |
| 4XL | $960 | 16-core ARM | Dedicated | 64 GB | 480 | 3,000 |
| 8XL | $1,870 | 32-core ARM | Dedicated | 128 GB | 490 | 6,000 |

## Disk Storage

### General Purpose (default)
- 8 GB included, then $0.125/GB
- 3,000 IOPS included, then $0.024/IOPS
- 125 MB/s throughput included, then $0.095 per MB/s
- Max size: 16 TB
- Durability: 99.9%

### High Performance
- $0.195/GB (no included amount)
- $0.119/IOPS
- Throughput scales automatically with IOPS
- Max size: 60 TB
- Durability: 99.999%
